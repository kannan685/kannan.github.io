Quick EcoTips

Small changes, big results. Try one this week.

01
Switch to a bamboo toothbrushOver a billion plastic toothbrushes end up in landfills every year. Bamboo biodegrades in 3–6 months.
02
Eat one plant-based meal a dayShifting just one meal reduces your dietary carbon footprint by up to 35% over the course of a year.
03
Unplug devices on standbyStandby power accounts for 10% of household energy bills. Use a smart power strip to cut it automatically.
04
Carry a reusable water bottleThe average person uses 156 plastic bottles a year. A reusable bottle pays back its carbon cost in under 2 weeks.
05
Buy second-hand firstBefore purchasing new, check local thrift stores or apps like Vinted or ThredUp. Fashion is responsible for 10% of global emissions.
06
Start a kitchen compost binFood waste in landfills produces methane, a gas 25x more potent than CO₂. Composting is one of the highest-impact changes you can make at home.
